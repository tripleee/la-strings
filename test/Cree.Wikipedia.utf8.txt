ᓃᔥᑕᒻᐹᔅᑌᒋᓂᑲᓐ
ᓀᐦᐃᔭᐧᐁᒧᐧᐃᓐ ᐧᐃᑭᐱᑎᔭ ᐄᔨᔫᔥᑌᐤ Uikuepitiu innushteu'
163 ᐹᔅᑌᒋᓂᑲᓇᓐ

In Standard Roman Orthographic Script:
Âpihtâkosisânak (Âpihtâkosiânak ahpô Otipeyimisowak) otânisa ekwa
okosisa Nehiyawa, Nahkawiyiniwa, Nâtawewa iskwewak ekwa Wemistikôsiwa,
Âkayâsiwa nâpewak. Wîkiwak Kânâtahk ekwa Kichi-môhkomânaskîhk.
Nehiyaw-isîhcikewin kîwikimew wemistikôsîw-isîhcikewina ekwa
kînihtâwikihew âpihtâkosisân-isîhcikewina. Opîkiskwewina âkayâsîmowin,
wemistikôsîmowin, âpihtâkosisân’wemistikôsîmowin, Michif ekwa
Bungee. Anohc, namôya awiyak pîkiskwa Bungee.
Âpihtâkosisânak ayâwak ayinânew ôtenawa ochi Alberta;
Mostos-Sâkahikan, Mekiwin-Sâkahikan (Utikuma), Pimiskâkan-Maskotew,
Sâkâstenohk-Maskotew, Kichi-Maskotew, Kikino, Elizabeth, ekwa
Kinosewesâhahikan. Nema askiy 1.28 mâwaci-kihci-mitâtahtomitanaw
