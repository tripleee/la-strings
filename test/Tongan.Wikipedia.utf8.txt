Ko e kotoa ʻo ha'a tangata ʻoku fānauʻi mai ʻoku tauʻatāina pea tatau
ʻi he ngeia mo e ngaahi totonu. Naʻe fakanāunauʻi kinautolu ʻaki ʻa e
ʻatamai mo e konisenisi pea ʻoku totonu ke nau feohi ʻi he laumālie ʻo
e nofo fakatautehina.
Ko e lahi ʻo e ngaahi kupu kotoa he taimí ni: 1,644.
Kau ʻetita fie liliu ʻa e ngaahi kupu ʻi he lea fakapilitānia ki he
lea fakatonga, vakai ki he hokohoko ʻi he Fale fakataha.
