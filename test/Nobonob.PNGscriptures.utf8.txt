Da Paulus, Kayak Nug da onilp̱a eum, da Kayak oo niiṯe bia Kristus 
Jesusnu totol danab daaṯem, da amu
O lailad, da anuḵa haen laa ahilagp̱a uḵe, Kayak nai genab amu mehuqi 
doop̱ig amge da nai laala enanag
Genab ig kristen ig Kristusnu doyak aomut amge o lailad da ag amelagp̱a 
nai mehuqmi amu da danab Kayak
Aria ag ihinignu inam dab meḵulag am ena. “Ag am Kristusnu uḏat danab, 
Kayak nug ig aḏi heḵunignu nuhig
Aria laa ag nai genab maṯinna, ag Korint ag oolagp̱a gap̱ai kobol nau 
daaṯe aeg doomi. Gap̱ai kobol
Aria ag oolagp̱a laa nug layagnu nai ele doyaya, nug kristen tamaniak 
bap̱aidḵulagnu dab ii meṯe. Iiṯa,
Gemu da nai imup̱a ag ad uḏug meeg, dahilp̱a doum amu da iiḵu nob meṯem. 
Danab laa nug nug awak ii heḵu
Aria dimiṯimnu danab, ag oolagp̱a genab ii dooṯeb ele amu ag doḏ main 
kayak ham bup̱uak medṯeb amu ig
Da hep̱i danab laa eheḏp̱a gomanu dab meṯem amunu ag da ḏo waap̱a dayeye 
heṯem bia dooṯebte? Anam iiṯa,
O lailad, ig alanḵad ag keeke anidp̱ig ele amu, da ag amunu dab mena 
oiglagnu ool daaṯe. Ig alanḵad
Am da Kristus Nug kobol aḏi aḏi heum ele amu andeṯe, da kobol amu dim 
lamidṯem, amubia ag ele da aniḏnana
Aria keeke laa imu ele iiḵu yaaḵulnu heṯem. O lailad, Kayak Ouḏi Nug 
uḏat diigdiig haknu ehah megṯe
Da danab amelagp̱a danab nailag diigdiig amu engel nailagp̱a ele madiṯem 
dayeb amge da laa dilagnu oo
Oo mauhaknu kobol anam daaṯe amunu ag hanhan danab ah laa dilag oolag 
mauhḵunu wagai menana, aria Kayak
O lailad, da ahilag dab mak, Nai Enanu, maḏ edaḵulnu heṯem. Da tatam
