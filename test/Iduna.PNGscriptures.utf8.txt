Yo siule tobohiyakweyao moya'aimi Kolinita againe. Iya Folo Yaubada 
givegideku be gave'afositolo. E,
Siule tobohiyakweyao miKolinita, tuwaina omi agaimiya ga'ela be Yaubada 
yana venuwa'ata Keliso fa'ine
Tobohiyakweyao, omi vaita we'owe'o amine, keke Yaubada 
wadahalamane-hagihagiyeni. Tuwaina buye kamiyami
Tu ime yami tovehawala ma fa'imeya ka'i gavaimi wananuwanuwa? 
Wananuwanuwa vaita ime Keliso yana tofaisewa
Atu venuwa'ata koyona ganogalina, higa kaliva sa'eyana omi tovetumagana 
ami yo'o agaimiya tamana vavinena
Tu tobohiyakweyao kadu ganagahe. Ka'i tamo hakwadi omi tovetumagana 
agaimiya tobohiya buye wanavekwageya
Siule tobohiyakweyao, yami toliluvaluva yaku leta againe 
wa'etoladina-ma ganafatana. Ka'i kaliva keke
Siule tobohiyakweyao, yami toliluvaluva sa'eyana ganogalina-ma kadu 
ganafatana, higa kevakeva kilumina
Tobohiyakweyao, kadu iya tauku fa'ikuya ganagahe. Wa'ita, keke tamo 
tomiyababi againe gadave'agetoga-yo
Siule tobohiyakweyao, nuwanuwaku bwanenedeyao yadi huluva fa'ina nuwami 
ganatalafolena. Badi gahe keke
Iya Keliso tavagine ganunudadana amine, meda anafaiweya omi iya 
tavagikuya wananunudadana.
Siule tobohiyakweyao miKolinita, Alu'aluwa Mahalina yana abavetala 
tulina tulina fa'ina ganakivetuvetunumi.
Ka'i adafaiweya tomiyababi meyadi tulina tulina agaidiya kanalukahihi 
kadu lobama ana tovenuwa'ata meyadiya
Tobohiyakweyao, wanavedivediwe be nuwanuwa hawahawahegana againe nuwami 
wanase-mo'ena. Kadu wanahawahegemi
