Kara Polkɨn, kara Krais Jisɨs siir aposel inkamkɨn. Kariirɨn Adi Komii 
siir kwoɨnkar naɨngwo okmwɨn
Pol siya boɨn, tɨ nomiiyauɨm, ɨiya kara kɨmiirɨm nɨta kara Adi Komii 
siir yai mɨiyɨka saiir boɨnmɨmauuwɨm
Tɨ nomiiyauɨm, bɨiya kara boɨnmauuwɨm yaiyɨm mɨ kara kɨmiirɨn kamɨm Adi 
Komii siir naeyɨugakɨm sɨmiir
Kɨma krɨmiir dɨnoknɨnkɨn, krɨma Krais siir mɨiyan inkamkɨm, siya 
krɨmiirɨn mɨnam nɨmbinkɨn Adi Komii
Yaiya bɨdi haigirɨrɨrkiya, hɨi nanaeni siyɨuɨn ɨni kɨmiir waraur 
nwowɨn. Tɨkɨ siyɨu hɨriinanɨn yɨo ɨni
Mɨ kɨmiir inkam nwɨrkɨ siya maɨrgɨmaɨrga siir nomiiyau nwɨrni nwo 
karamae nwowi, yɨo wɨ yaigak nwowi.
Mɨ ta kara wɨnkiya yokwoni kɨmiiram kara mɨn swokɨ wɨn boɨnkikɨn, tɨ 
digworaekwowɨm kɨma srɨigiyɨm,
Mɨ dimɨn taemɨnɨm panao kara kɨmiiram wɨnkii, kara pɨ dimɨn taemɨnɨm 
iikamɨm yɨpɨkɨ tɨ nu ɨiir nwowɨm
Kariirɨn wɨ dimɨn whɨrkɨ nɨkɨdu rani, tɨ dimɨnɨm kara tɨriyɨm Adi Komii 
siirɨm. Kara aposel inkamɨn
Tɨ kariir nomiiyauɨm kara ha naɨngwowi kɨma ɨna sɨbgu ɨnoknɨnkɨnɨm 
siyɨuɨn Adi Komii siya tɨrɨn krɨmiir
Kɨma tɨ dimɨn taemɨnɨm kara tɨriyɨm sɨmiir kɨgna ap, tɨ kara mɨ kɨgna 
apiyɨm dimɨn taemɨnɨm Krais siya
Tɨ kariir nomiiyauwɨm kara tɨ yai ɨmiirɨn mɨnam naɨngwo wɨnkikɨn kɨma 
tɨ dimɨn taemɨnɨm Adi Komii siir
Mɨ kara iikamɨm omom omomanɨm sɨmiir yaigɨn ɨmiir boɨni mɨ paekwosɨmi 
yaigɨn ɨkɨm mɨ kara nhɨrmiyam
Mɨ iikam kɨma bɨikɨbɨiyɨn iikam nhɨrmiyam daɨngwokwo. Yɨo tɨriigɨnɨn 
kougɨ kouwɨn ɨni siya ywowɨn. Mɨ
Mɨ tariinanɨn tɨ kariir nomiiyauɨm, kara mɨnan naɨngwowi, kɨma ta yai 
wɨn kɨbiya yɨpɨkɨ bɨigɨ bɨi kara
