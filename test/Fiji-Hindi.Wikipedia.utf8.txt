Leonidas, pahila jahaaj rahaa jisme girmitya logan ke India se Fiji
lawa gais rahaa. Isse pahile ii jahaaj West Indies me girmitya log ke
lai jae ke khatir kaam me lawa gais rahaa. Leonidas, 3 March 1879 ke
Calcutta chorris aur 72 din baad, 15 May 1879, ke Levuka
pahuchis. Jahaaj me kul jorr 498 passenger rahin jisme 273 admi, 146
aurat aur 79 larrkan rahin. Leonidas ke Calcutta chorre ke khali tiin
din baad kuch girmitya log ke haeja (cholera) aur kuch ke maata
(smallpox) pakarr liis aur 17 log mar gain. Fiji pahuche pe Yanuca
Lailai ke quarantine station banawa gais jahan pe 15 aur log
dysentery, diarrhoea aur typhoid se mar gain. Kuchh din ke baad,
bachaa girmitya logan ke plantation pe bheje ke khatir, Levuka lawa
gais lekin khaali ek planter, Rabi ke J. Hill, 52 admii, 25 aurat aur
29 larrkan ke liis. Kuchh ke ghar me kaam pe lagawa gais lekin sab se
jaada log (189 admii aur 97 aurat) ke sarkar ke public works me kaam
me lagawa gais. (aur...)
Royal Fiji Military Force (RFMF) ke 3FIR ke pahile ke Commander
Lieutenant Colonel Ratu Tevita Uluilakeba Mara, jon ki Fiji ke pahile
ke Prime Minister aur President, Ratu Mara ke larrka hae, ke Fiji se
bhaage ke khatir ek fugitive declare karaa gais.
1879 se lae ke 1916 tak, 42 jahaaj, 87 trip me 60,553 girmitya logan
ke lae ke Fiji se aais rahaa. Isme se 45,439 log Calcutta se aur
15,114 log Madras se jahaaj me charrhin rahaa.
Karela ek faile waala paudha hai jiske Hindustani log tarkari banaae
hai. Ii garam des me hoe hai. Iske tarkari karrua rahe hai. 
Ii panna ke 30 March 2011, ke 10:36 pichhla time badla gais rahaa.
Saal ke khalaas hoe talak, Fiji me 44,220 Hindustani rahin, jisme se
14,462 girmit ke niche rahin. Ii saal me 4,204 girmitya logan ke Fiji
lawa gais rahaa, jisme se 2,233, Calcutta se, aur 1,971, Madras se
aawe rahin. 708 gimitya logan India laut gae rahin.
