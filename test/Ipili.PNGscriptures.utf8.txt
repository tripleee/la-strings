toto, namba ee lo meane. Wamba Goteto
1-2 Amene imalini yame, namba yakama ateainga epewa-angi, nambato 
Gotena pii wamba too pitane ateane tupa
Amene imalini ima, wamba yakamato Oli Sipitisa oko wayumane mina wato 
napene, yakamana nembo-tene wambane
Wandakali-mane nanima aposolo akali tupa nembo toto, nanima Kataisana 
piape akali tupa atoto, balana
Nambato ale ewa-kola, wandakali-mane Kotini tano okona tane Kitisene 
akali yakamana mindimane bala ayiane
1-2 Matili yuu oko koyo pula-angi, Gotena wandakali yakamato bala lo 
bilipi na-lalaini wandakali tupa pitaka
Wamba yakamato nambana pepa mindi peyo epe loto, pii minditupa namba 
tipa peai okonena, andipa nambato
1-4 Aitene wandakali-mane minjuni tupa owato gote mindi lo opa maiyu, 
matili salimi leyai-kola, Kitisene
1-3 Wandakali minditupa-mane namba yapa lo tale puato, bala aposolo 
akali enene mindi ata napeya lalaini.

tupa pitaka moyoyale mangene kambua

mana utupane wato minalapape.
