Nyo Pol, Krais Jisas fikoo bus vanyo ma faarei anyo na amaraav Tsunia, 
tan koman ten Gov. Amam me Sostenes,
Fakats vanyo yam faarei rou a ka iny faatok mami tan nainy te ruak mi 
nyo nei tsumin vamuinyasiny fafaaman
Sana min vamuinyasiny fafaaman tsonyo, tan nainy te kaakaa fiisen mi 
nyo mi na aya, nyo gima onot ma
A sanaan to ayei, ma tagei fi mamam a vainy to, amam tsoiny binun ten 
Krais, to te tatagaa ot iny rom
A isen a mes te tsue of vanyo na ka to, kaa minon a isen a mes 
fapoopoan nana gum iny vainy fafaaman
Nyo komainy kirkir pis of maromi na mes a patang te kaa miromi naa to: 
nainy te fatsitsien minon a isen
Koman noun te faan iny mimi tsonyo, mi rangats vatuanyo na foka, sai 
tovei nyo tsue tsuk iny rou a foka
Tovei, nyo komainy biny rou rangat tsumi to te onot fi rorin vainy 
fafaaman ma ainy tu venoo to te kat
Mesapan te tsue na ka nyo gima faarei na amaraav faman, tana saa nyo 
gima nom foiny rou faarei rarou
Tovei nyo komainy tsue pis rou nyo te biny fatabiny pis a fo rangat 
tsumi to te toobing fi non a mes
To sana mi ma tagaa tana sanaan te kaakaa fi ronyo tovei, am vovou iny 
towa faarei te katkat fi nyo
Nyo farong pis rou vegiau nyo te biny a fo rangat tsumi tana sanaan to 
te bibinun fi non Aaven Taabos
Ana sanaan tovei, te mangiir fi non a mes, a mes a vainy, tana saa 
kainon to te vegiau fi ronyon a fo
Ai to te karap fi non kat to ayei, ito te mangiir fi romi na mesmes, mi 
ma vaputs fiisen me na aavem
Sai tovei vamuinyasiny fafaaman tsonyo, nyo komainy tsue fanat of pis 
maromi tan Vurungan Rof nane Krais
