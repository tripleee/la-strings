1-2 'Abo'agu Yaubada 'iesinuwegu be 'aene Yesu Keliso tetelina 'ana 
tosimana, 'omi me Kolinita toekalesiyao
Tasigwao, wanuwa'i'isi tuta nina lowa Yaubada tetelina yame'ena ga 
yasimanena 'emiya, nigeya 'aene 'abo'agu
Ta tasigwao, nigeya kapekapeyana be mwalatoi 'ai'aila yasimana 'emiya, 
manuna yaluyaluwami nigeya simatumatuwa,
Ta esi 'abo'ama Keliso 'ina toguinuwao, manuna tetela nina Yaubada 
'enega lowa kwaikwaiyina ta gete
'Imi to'umalina yanonona manuna 'ebweu tai 'emiyega tamana mwanena 
ma'iyana siloegesi. Loegesi nate
'Ebweu wate yage 'ebe omayamaya 'emiya 'imiyami, nate 'imi etala 'ediya 
toloinao nigeya totapwalolo
Nuwanuwa nina ai manuna ni'atu 'igu leta 'enaya wasimanena, 'enega 
'abo'agu yaeonami 'aene bobo'ana
'Ebweu wate enaida 'igu leta 'enaya ni'atu wasimanenaya, nate masula 
nidi tomota si'ebwa'ebwaya 'idi
Geya'abo tomota sitootoo be siloinegu, nai silomulomugiyegu, ta esi 
wanuwa'i'isi 'aene 'abo'agu 'Inapwana
Gosegwao tubudao wada nuwa'i'isidi 'aene lowaenei tuta nina sena mwatui 
daitaya sitautauya, ta pwana
'Ilobwenemi be 'igu bubuna gete waetotowane, nadigega 'abo'agu Keliso 
'ina bubuna yaetotowanena.
Gosegwao, nuwanuwagu be Yaubada 'ina eguyai yaluyaluwada manudi 
yali'ami be wada mwalatoni.
'Eguma 'igu onao bobo'adi, 'atuwa tologuguyao be aniloseyao 'idi ona, 
ta 'abo oboboma nigeya 'ateguya
'Enega 'ilobweneda be tatoo waiwai be tada eobooboboma 'ai'aila, ta 
wate tada toowaiwai be Yaubada Yaluyaluwana
1-2 Gosegwao, tetela nina ni'atu yasimanena 'emiya, wanuwa'i'isi 'aene 
lowa 'atemi watagwalena tetela nina
