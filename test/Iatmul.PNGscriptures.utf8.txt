Wun Pol wun. Wan pas wunayɨ pɨlɨwutɨga. God ndɨna maawupmba wandɨ maa 
aposelat tɨwun. Mbambala Jisas
Kɨta mbaapma nɨmba, tamba ngwuk kat God waa nyaangɨt kiyawun. Nɨma 
kaawa viyaga nɨma kwundimba waga
Kɨta mbaapma nɨmba, ngwutnogwinala lɨwa njɨmbal Godna waagan maawupmba 
tɨndɨ, ta nɨmba kat kbutɨwa nyaangɨt
Wan apma nyaangɨt ngwuk kat mbutɨga nɨmbo, nɨno, nɨn kat wupma yelavɨk 
ngwula. Ndi kɨta vat Kraistna
Yambɨsɨk yilɨngwa vat mbuka kalilɨndɨ, wutwun. Wan yilɨngwa vat kavle 
glei vatna. Kraistna mbaapmamba
Ngwutna kɨta nyan ndɨna kɨta mbaapmamba lɨga nyan kat kɨmbut yiga lɨga 
ndɨ kat kwutaa kotmak kalingeyan
Wun kat kwingwa pasmba nɨma sakwat wagalangwa nyaangɨt tɨndɨ, vɨwun. 
Ngwuk kat awat wangwa nyaangɨk
Mbambala ngwuk kat waagan kat kwinja mbatna nyaangɨt wun kat wagalangwa 
nyaangɨt awat ngwuk kat mbupmat
Kɨta ngepma nɨmba yetɨga vatna aitamba ana lɨgowun. Wun aposel wun. 
Nɨna njambwi nyan Jisas kat wuna
Yakwa nɨmba kɨta vat vɨngwangat wowun. Tamba Moses ta njɨmbla tɨmbu 
yinja yambɨ ndi kat vɨsɨmogwindɨ.
Kraist yeta vla yetɨgowun. Ngi kat tɨga wunai yeta vla yetɨ ngwula.
Mbambala ngwuk kat Godna waagan kwilɨga vak kat ngwuk kat mbupmat 
yigowun. Wuna mbaapmamba lɨga nɨmba
Kapma kapma ngepma kwundimba ngambuga nyɨnangwupmba lɨga enselna ngepma 
kwundimb ngambuga nat nɨmba
Nat nɨmba kat nɨma maawut yelavɨka lɨngweya vak klavak kat yelavɨk 
ngwula. Godna waagan kwilɨga vak
Wuna mbaapmamba lɨga nɨmba ngwuk kat tak mbukwa apma nyaangɨt nat 
njambɨ ak ngwuk kat mbupmat yigowun.
