Naeba Porona, Karaisiti Isu nkaposoro Koti nkabemika unatantiye. Piya, 
naebe tao'yagara Sositenisibe,
Nao'mitaba, naeba tigeti irosa'ma Koti nkage awaga kamana urimintopa, 
aogi napiyenanetasabe tabe kamana'netasabe
We, nao'mitaba, Awamusa waintaga kina'mi kamanapa uwaimintopa, pabiyama 
tigepa paipa kampa urimintone.
Pigoya, tigeba taeka napimagiri: Karaisiti kayokaya kina mima, Koti 
kakanta kamana'ama nkago aborakena
Tige tikapi kumiyenaba tabera aborama waintiye, yega abuwe. Piya, 
ka'waina abawampa nkwae maeye, yega
Tigetisa ka'waina ao'mika amo kamana otamipa, aeba Kotiti atokaeya 
kinapa iyemita puma, abe'isa kina'mi
Tigeba esa namintiri, kamana nabigataga, pika mayama aborakiri abiyo: 
Ka yagarama waya kampa maenta
Pigoya, ama kotiti kaema agunta pasa'ataka naeba kamana ukena puwe. 
Piya, taeba tonarona napiyenaba
Pigoya, ka'enama naraku'ma ke'nepa akuniya, a. Naeba aposoroba kampa 
mintuwa, a. Naeba Wa'egare Isupa
Nao'mitaba, a'yugu pintanamaka kampa napisanaga ma kamanapa urimukuwa, 
abiyo: Pai Koti nkibinankama
Eya, naeba Karaisiti ki aegaraosa puma, tigeba pabiyama nae ki 
naegaraiyo.
Nao'mitaba, Awamusa'ama aboraramintintanaga aibosabo pusanaga 
urimukuwa, abiyo:
Pi tiyakikubo kipa mayamawe: Piya, naeba agu kao'ena kampa punta, 
tonarona wasanami kamanapisabe enisore
Tigeba agu kao'enayaga mikenaga ebi ebi pumagiri, Awamusa'ama 
aboraramemi ntuga pukenaga napiyiyo. Eya,
